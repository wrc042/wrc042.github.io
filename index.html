Ruicheng Wang

I am an incoming PhD student at Georgia Tech, supervised by Prof. Bo Zhu. My research interests lie in Physics-Based Animation and Computer Graphics, focusing on developing high-performance and high-fidelity numerical algorithms for simulating physical phenomena like fluids, solids, continuum materials and more.


Previously, I was a research intern in Visual Computing and Learning Lab, advised by Prof. Baoquan Chen and Dr. Bin Wang. From Sep. 2021 to Oct. 2022, I was a research intern in Embodied Perception and InteraCtion (EPIC) Lab, advised by Prof. He Wang. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Yuanpei College, Peking University in 2024.


I was also the team member of the Peking University Supercomputing Team, and we won the First Place at the 10th ASC and the Second Place at SC23.
